WHAT WE DO

Six disciplines. One standard.

Every service is delivered by trained, uniformed, and vetted personnel, backed by supervisors and a monitoring desk that never sleeps.

Manned Guarding

Trained, uniformed guards stationed at homes, offices, and institutions, providing visible deterrence and rapid response.

Event Security Management

VIP protection and crowd control for concerts, conferences, and private functions, planned around the specific risk profile of each event.

Cash-in-Transit & Escort

Secure transport of cash and valuables between premises, banks, and ATMs, with trained escort teams and tracked vehicles.

CCTV Installation & Monitoring

Smart surveillance systems designed, installed, and monitored remotely, with footage retention and real-time alerting.

Access Control Solutions

Biometric and digital entry systems for controlled access to buildings, offices, and restricted zones.

Private Investigation & Risk Management

Discreet investigative services and risk assessments to identify vulnerabilities before they become incidents.
